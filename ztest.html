Adjective

  • of, relating to, or forming a base
  • fundamental
  • a basic principle
  • the basic ingredient
  • pertaining to, of the nature of, or containing a base
  • not having all of the hydroxyls of the base replaced by the acid group, or having the metal or its equivalent united partly to the acid group and partly to oxygen
  • noting, pertaining to, or made by a steelmaking process
  • having relatively little silica
  • characterized by predictable or unoriginal style, interests, or behavior
  • boringly predictable or unoriginal

Noun

  • basic training
  • a soldier or airman receiving basic training
  • something that is fundamental or basic; an essential ingredient, principle, procedure, etc.
  • a person, especially a female, who is boringly predictable or unoriginal
